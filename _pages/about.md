Hendra Bunyamin is a lecturer who graduated from Mathematics department Bandung Institute of Technology and Software Engineering, Informatics department from the same university.     
    
He is very passionate about teaching. Mainly, he teaches mathematics, artificial intelligence, and programming.    
   
His research interests are applying machine learning algorithms into various problems.      
    
During his undergraduate study, he studied Statistics under [Prof. Sutawanir Darwis](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=ojCW8aIAAAAJ). His undergraduate final project was about spatial prediction using [Kriging](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kriging) which was also known as __Gaussian process regression__.     

He took his master study in Software Engineering. Furthermore, his master thesis explored [__Latent Semantic Indexing__](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Latent_semantic_analysis).
